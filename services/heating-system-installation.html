Heating System Options

High-Efficiency Furnaces

Modern gas and electric furnaces provide reliable, efficient heating for homes with existing ductwork. We install ENERGY STAR certified furnaces with AFUE ratings up to 98%, ensuring maximum energy savings and consistent warmth.

Heat Pump Systems

Heat pumps provide both heating and cooling from a single system, offering exceptional efficiency and year-round comfort. These systems are ideal for Coldstream's moderate climate and can significantly reduce energy costs.

Ductless Heating Solutions

Ductless mini split heat pumps provide targeted heating for specific areas without requiring extensive ductwork. These systems offer precise temperature control and exceptional efficiency for zone heating applications.

Radiant Heating Systems

Radiant floor heating provides comfortable, even heat distribution throughout your home. These systems operate silently and efficiently, creating comfortable living spaces without the air movement associated with forced-air systems.
