Common Mini Split Problems We Repair

Refrigerant Leaks

Refrigerant leaks are among the most common issues affecting mini split performance. Our technicians use advanced leak detection equipment to locate even small leaks, then perform professional repairs using approved materials and techniques. We properly evacuate and recharge the system to restore optimal cooling and heating capacity.

Electrical Component Failures

Complex electrical systems control modern ductless units, and component failures can cause various operational issues. Our certified electricians diagnose and replace failed capacitors, contactors, thermistors, and control boards using genuine OEM parts to ensure reliable, long-lasting repairs.

Compressor Problems

Compressor issues require specialized expertise and diagnostic equipment. We perform comprehensive compressor testing, including electrical measurements and refrigerant analysis, to determine whether repair or replacement is the most cost-effective solution for your specific situation.

Drainage and Condensate Issues

Blocked drainage systems can cause water damage and reduce indoor air quality. Our technicians thoroughly clean condensate drains, inspect drain pans, and ensure proper drainage slope to prevent future blockages and water-related problems.

Fan Motor Failures

Both indoor and outdoor fan motors are critical for proper system operation. We replace failed motors with high-quality replacements, ensuring proper airflow and efficient heat transfer. Our repairs include proper electrical connections and balanced operation testing.

Our Comprehensive Diagnostic Process

Initial System Assessment

Every repair begins with a thorough assessment of your system's overall condition and performance. Our technicians examine both indoor and outdoor units, checking for obvious signs of damage, wear, or improper installation that might contribute to current problems or future failures.

Advanced Diagnostic Testing

We utilize professional-grade diagnostic equipment to measure system performance parameters including refrigerant pressures, electrical values, airflow rates, and temperature differentials. This comprehensive testing approach enables accurate problem identification and prevents unnecessary part replacements.

Component-by-Component Analysis

Our systematic diagnostic approach examines each system component individually, testing electrical connections, mechanical operation, and performance specifications. This detailed analysis ensures we identify all issues affecting your system, not just the most obvious symptoms.

Root Cause Identification

Beyond addressing immediate symptoms, we investigate underlying causes that may have contributed to component failures. This approach prevents recurring problems and helps extend your system's overall lifespan through proper maintenance and operational adjustments.
